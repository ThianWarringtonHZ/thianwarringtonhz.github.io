Frequently Asked Questions

How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ?

Visit hz.mynetpay.nl and add some balance to your account. Then upload the documents you want to print. In the HZ building, hold your student pass next to the printer to start printing the current queue of documents.

How can you scan a document and send it to your laptop at HZ?

Authenticate at the printer with your student pass. Choose option 'Scan' and lay the papers on the glass plate. Then press start.

How can I buy something (like when I sign up for the IT introduction event) on the HZ web shop?

Visit the HZ webshop via this link. Then click on the item you want to purchase and complete the checkout.

How can you book a project space in one of the wings?

Visit the HZ Selfservice Portal. Then click on 'Make a reservation' and then 'Book a room'.

What are the instructions if you want to park your car at the HZ parking lot?

If you want to park your car at the HZ parking lot, you have to visit the reception. They can activate your student pass to give access to Rijkswaterstaats' parking lot. There you can park your car at one of the HZ parking places.
